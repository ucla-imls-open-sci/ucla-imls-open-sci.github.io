**Launching the Curriculum Hub: Lessons for Librarians in Open Science**

We are proud to release version 1.0.0 of the "Lessons for Librarians in Open Science Principles and Methods" project. This release marks the completion of our IMLS-funded grant and the launch of our new curriculum hub.

**Live Resources:**
*   **Curriculum Portal:** [https://ucla-imls-open-sci.info](https://ucla-imls-open-sci.info)
*   **GitHub Organization:** [https://github.com/ucla-imls-open-sci](https://github.com/ucla-imls-open-sci)

**Abstract:**
This archive contains the source code and curriculum materials for a comprehensive, open-source training program designed to empower librarians with skills in Open Science principles, data management, reproducibility, and community building. Developed by 15 teams of librarian-authors and supported by the UCLA Library Data Science Center.

**Key Features of this Release:**
*   **15 Open Science Lessons:** Covering topics from "Intro to Containers" to "Open Qualitative Research".
*   **Centralized Portal:** A fully migrated Astro v5 static site serving as the curriculum hub.
*   **Standardized Metadata:** Individual `CITATION.cff` files for all lesson repositories.
*   **Team Directory:** Dynamic profiles for authors and committee members with ORCID integration.
